Some of My Favorite Movies

Klaus

A very cute movie about a young man that becomes a mail carrier in the worst little town where the residents always fight. he finds an old man on the mountain and eventually befriends him, leading to the creation of Santa Claus.

Big Fish

A heartwarming story about a man with an astranged relationship with his father whom he thinks he knows nothing about. his father loves to tell extravagent stories about his life, too unbelieveable. His son finds out there is more truth to his stories than he thought.

The Last Unicorn

A classic tale, of a unicorn trying to find her way as the last in existance. With help from her friends made along the way she is able to hunt down who is responsible for the missing unicorns.
